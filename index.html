Kate Patterson

Associate Professor, Mathematics

Biography

A native of Lander, Wyoming, Kate Patterson joined Central Wyoming College in 2014. Before returning home, she spent three years as a research fellow at the University of Auckland in New Zealand.

Kate earned her Ph.D. in Mathematics from Montana State University-Bozeman and her B.S. from the University of Wyoming. She is dedicated to fostering "shared curiosity" in her classrooms, empowering students to explore the deep beauty of mathematical structures.
